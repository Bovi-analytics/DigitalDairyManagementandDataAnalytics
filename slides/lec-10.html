Lecture - Youngstock metrics

ANSCI 4040 – Dairy Data Analysis & Analytics

Ass. Prof. Dr. Miel Hostens

Learning goals

  • Define each youngstock reproductive KPI precisely
  • Add common synonyms/aliases
  • For each metric: reflect on bias, momentum, variance, lag
  • What question can each metric answer, or not
  • Identify minimum data fields needed to compute reliably

Youngstock KPIs: why they’re different

  • Heifers are not “DIM-based”; they’re age-based and growth/management dependent
  • Denominators are often small and strongly affected by:
    • enrollment criteria (eligible/not eligible)
    • missing heat detection and unrecorded services
    • pregnancy confirmation practices

Data prerequisites (heifers)

Minimum fields:

  • Birth date, ID, breed

  • First heat date (if observed/sensor)

  • First service date(s)

  • Pregnancy diagnosis date & outcome

  • Calving date (first calving) or removal date

Data prerequisites (heifers)

Optional but bias-reducing:

  • Body weight / ADG, BCS (if used), health events

  • Pen/group, breeding method (AI vs bull), semen ID

Age at first heat

  • Synonyms: age at puberty, age at first estrus

  • Definition: age (days or months) at first recorded estrus event.

  • Bias - Under-detection (no sensors, low observation intensity) - “First recorded” ≠ “first occurred”

  • Momentum - Reflects months of rearing decisions (nutrition, health) → slow to change

  • Variance - Small cohorts + seasonality in observation/sensors

  • Lag - Management change today shows up only when new calves reach puberty age

Age at first insemination

  • Synonyms: age at first service, age at first AI

  • Definition: age at first recorded insemination (AI or bull service if recorded).

  • Bias - Eligibility rules (min BW/age) differ by farm - Unrecorded natural services inflate “late” ages

  • Momentum - Strongly driven by growth program (pipeline)

  • Variance - Clustered by breeding season / staffing

  • Lag - Nutrition changes → growth → later first service effects

Age at pregnancy

  • Synonyms: age at conception (often approximated), age at confirmed pregnancy

  • Definition (recommended): - age at pregnancy confirmation date - optionally infer conception date = AI date + 0 (for AI-based conception)

  • Bias - Delayed pregnancy diagnosis shifts ages later - Pregnancy loss misclassified if not rechecked

  • Momentum - Influenced by both service timing and conception success

  • Variance - Small n; strong clustering by bull/tech

  • Lag - Program changes impact after heifers reach breeding eligibility

Expected calving age

  • Synonyms: predicted calving age, due-date age

  • Definition: age at expected calving date = pregnancy date + gestation length (standardized or breed-specific).

  • Bias - If pregnancy date is “confirmation date,” due date is biased late unless corrected - Missing or incorrect gestation length assumptions

  • Momentum - Mirrors age at pregnancy with additional fixed lag

  • Variance - Minimal beyond pregnancy date noise

  • Lag - Inherent gestation lag

Number of inseminations per heifer

  • Synonyms: services per heifer, service intensity

  • Definition: total services recorded for heifers in a defined cohort/time window.

  • Bias - Missing service records (especially natural service) - Heifers sold/culled mid-process (censoring)

  • Momentum - Will improve slowly; reflects both estrus detection and fertility

  • Variance - High at small n; outliers dominate (repeat breeders)

  • Lag - Responds after enough heifers complete breeding cycle

Number of heifers pregnant

  • Synonyms: pregnant heifer inventory

  • Definition: count of heifers with confirmed pregnancy (cross-sectional).

  • Bias - Depends on pregnancy check schedule and recording - Pregnancy loss not updated → overcount

  • Momentum - Inventory responds slowly (pipeline)

  • Variance - Lower if herd is large; higher for small operations

  • Lag - Built-in due to breeding + preg-check delay

Number of youngstock not served > 16 months

  • Synonyms: overdue heifers, eligible-not-bred

  • Definition: count of heifers older than threshold (e.g., 16 mo) with no recorded service.

  • Bias - Missing service records; unrecorded bull exposure - “Eligibility” should ideally include weight/health, not just age

  • Momentum - Sensitive to backlog (can change fast if you “catch up”)

  • Variance - Strongly seasonal if breeding is seasonal

  • Lag - Low (operational metric)

Age at first calving

  • Synonyms: AFC (age at first calving)

  • Definition: age at first calving date.

  • Bias - Sold/cull heifers excluded (survivorship bias) - Induced calving / abortion not handled consistently

  • Momentum - Highly “sticky”: reflects months/years of upstream decisions

  • Variance - Moderate; depends on cohort size

  • Lag - Long (breeding + gestation)

Conception rate first insemination (heifers)

  • Synonyms: first-service conception rate, P/AI at 1st service

  • Definition: among heifers receiving first service, proportion confirmed pregnant from that service.

  • Bias - Pregnancy diagnosis timing and loss confound “conception” - If rechecks aren’t done, early loss misclassified as failure or success

  • Momentum - Can change faster than AFC but still requires preg-check pipeline

  • Variance - High when few first services occur

  • Lag - At least until pregnancy confirmation

Total number of pregnancies (heifers)

  • Synonyms: total heifer pregnancies, preg count

  • Definition: total confirmed pregnancies in a time window (cross-sectional or cohort).

  • Bias - Double counting if rechecks create duplicate pregnancy events - Under-count if no confirmation recorded

  • Momentum - Inventory-like

  • Variance - Depends on heifer flow

  • Lag - Driven by pregnancy diagnosis schedule

Total number of inseminations (heifers)

  • Definition: total recorded services for heifers over a period.

  • Bias - Missing recording, bull exposure, duplicate entries

  • Momentum - Responds quickly to management changes (technique, scheduling)

  • Variance - High if only a few breeding days

  • Lag - Minimal (event metric)

Implementation notes (heifer dashboard)

Recommended visuals:

  • Cohort waterfall: birth → first service → pregnant → calved

  • Histogram: age at first service, age at pregnancy, AFC

  • “Overdue” list: >16 mo not served (action list)
